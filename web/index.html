Standard DJ

  • This package includes personalised playlists, individual to each customer and function. Tracks are mixed live, and requests are welcome throughout the evening. Karaoke is also available upon request at the event.
  • This standard package also includes lights and personalised visuals unique to the occasion.

    • Prices start from £130.

Live Music

This packages includes everything listed in the Standard DJ Package.

  • Solo saxophone performances of classics such as ‘Careless Whisper,’ ‘A Million Love Songs,’ and ‘Baker Street,’ are subject to a small extra cost.
  • Other live music performances are available subject to extra cost, as well as the availability of local bands and musicians.

Prices start from £150.

Photography

This packages includes everything listed in the Standard DJ Package.

  • This photography package is subject to the availability of external photography company - (Owen Peters Photography). Professional action shots will be taken throughout the function, and these images will be uploaded to Facebook which can be downloaded for free.
  • A Photo-booth is also available subject to extra cost and availability. This includes personalised frames and party props. These images will be posted straight to Facebook so you can download to your heart's content!

Prices start from £200.

Kids Package

  • This package includes personalised playlists containing songs from all of their favourite movies!
  • Popular party games of your choice can be played throughout the event and a lively, enjoyable atmosphere for both kids and parents alike is guaranteed!

Prices start from £60.

Prop Frames

  • Our brand new prop frame is perfect for any party / event. Take selfies inside the frame with your guests, and forever remember your party with your own personalised message on the frame!
  • This Add-On is available as a stand alone extra to your order, and can also be added to the Photography Package!

    • Price: £10

Presentation

  • Send us all of your favourite pictures with your guests before the event, and we will create a powerpoint presentation which will be displayed on a projector throughout the party!
  • This Add-On is available as a stand alone extra to your order, and can also be added to the Photography Package combined with the images taken at the function!

Price: £20

Scrapbook

  • Why not let us create a souvenir of your event. We will create a scrapbook with messages from your guests taken at the party, as well as pictures taken by our photographer on the evening.
  • Only available with the Photography Package! The scrapbook will be completed and posted within a week of the event!

Price: £20
